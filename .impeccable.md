# Design Context

- Target audience: Self-directed investors and small research teams scanning US and KR equities for high-conviction swing and position setups.
- Core use cases: Compare consensus rankings, monitor market regime risk, inspect why a ticker is passing or failing across multiple strategies, and move quickly from leaderboard to detailed analysis.
- Brand personality: Premium, disciplined, and slightly intimidating; closer to an institutional signal desk than a consumer finance app.
- Visual tone: Dark, dense, and calm with sharp information hierarchy, brass-and-teal accents, and an industrial research-terminal feel.
